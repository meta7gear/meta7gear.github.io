GCH CHEZNUTPEARL ZANZIBAR TRADITIONAL LINES

DNA tested HCM & PKD CLEAR

Colour: Seal True Bicolour carrying Chocolate and Dilute
Sire: Miwahni Tonga
Dam: Hazelwood Precious Sinead

Bio:

Zanzi is one of our younger Kings and a welcome member to the family, he is quite a big boy weighing 5kgs at 6 months and has fantastic boning, a truly gorgeous head with well set ears, superb blue eyes coupled with a plush coat and a fantastic sweet and affectionate temperament. He has now proven himself as a wonderful sire with his daughter Fabouki winning Ragdoll Kitten Of The Year from limited showing. He has been siring wonderful babies with gorgeous natures. We are so excited to have been given the opportunity to add him to our breeding program.
